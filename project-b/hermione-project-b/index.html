Resonant Arcana

In the year 3025, emotions are no longer fleeting whispers of the heart—they are quantifiable, stored, and algorithmically processed. Humanity has achieved unparalleled synergy with AI, yet amidst this evolution, the essence of feeling has grown distant, abstract... almost forgotten.

Dr. Enoim, a visionary scientist, developed the Emotion Cards as a bridge to rekindle this lost intimacy. Each card represents not just a feeling, but a gateway to rediscover it—tangibly, interactively, genuinely. This is not just a tool; it’s a reawakening.
