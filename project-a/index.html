[ As You See it, Feel it ]

Scientific Name:

Irradia

Discovered by / at:

Dr. Enoim, a ramdom evening

Family:

Irradia doesn't have a family. Actually, she randomly pops out from Dr. Enoim's mind, and is constantly seeking a sense of belonging, a shelter, but she is never satisfied.

Habitat:

Fathomight ( an abstraction of Dr. Enoim's mind, where the color changes according to the certain emotion )

Appearance:

Irradia resembles the human eye, with layers of skin that mimic the iris, each one uniquely distinct. She pulses, revealing the rhythm of her heartbeat and breath.

Key Characteristics:

  • Floats ranodmly.
  • Reacts sensitively.
  • Eats the energy of emotions.
  • Loves introspecting.

What we know so far about Irradia? The name Irradia, derived from the Latin word "irradiare" meaning "to shine," symbolizes the inner light that radiates through emotions. It represents the clarity that emotional awareness brings, illuminating and healing through emotional strength. Irradia is a collection and abstraction of the eyes that Dr. Enoim has deeply gazed into over the years. 'I find human eyes incredibly captivating,' Dr.Enoim explains. 'They unknowingly reveal emotions that words often fail to express, and they rarely lie.' It was this fascination that led to the creation of Irradia. 'She embodies that honesty,' Dr.Enoim continues. 'Irradia is a reflection of pure sincerity, an entity born from the very essence of truth that lies hidden within the human gaze.'
Guidelines for human interaction with Irradia According to Dr.Enoim, Irradia is curious however timid. It moves around randomly and slowly, and somehow reluctantly but sensitively reacts to humans' interaction. If you put your hands near it or drag it, it will drift towards you, but very slowly. But when you take your hand away, it will change its appearance as a way of defence. She does, however, welcome the opportunity to show you different versions of herself when you use a remote controller, as it allows her the space to introspect.
